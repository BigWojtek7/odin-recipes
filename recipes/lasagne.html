Lasagne

Description

This is an adaptation of the official lasagne from the Bolognese Chamber of Commerce. I've made a couple of changes suggested to me by people from the area, such as which meat and wine to use or if there should be milk added or not.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup diced carrot
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 ½ cups plain tomato sauce
  • 2 cups vegetable broth, or more as needed
  • ½ cup dry red wine

Steps

  1. Saute pancetta in a large stockpot over medium-high heat until fat has rendered, 1 to 2 minutes.
  2. Stir wine into the stockpot and bring to a boil
  3. Pour 1 more cup of broth into the sauce.
  4. Start preparing pasta after sauce has been cooking for 1 1/2 hours.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the sauce is bubbling and the cheese on top has browned, 25 to 30 minutes.
